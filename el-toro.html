El Toro

El Toro is an 8-foot pram sailed single-handedly and remains one of the Bay Area’s most familiar small-boat classes.

El Toro is an 8-foot pram which is sailed single handedly. The boat is the first boat for almost all kids in the Bay Area.

A very active social schedule complements an active racing schedule for the senior El Toro sailors.
