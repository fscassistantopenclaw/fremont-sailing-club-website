Fleets

Fremont Sailing Club supports several small-boat fleets, including Lido 14, El Toro, and an open fleet for other dinghies sailing with the club.

Fremont Sailing Club supports multiple small-boat fleets and welcomes a range of sailors and boat types at club events.

Lido The Lido 14 is a two-person boat with a mainsail and jib, known for its comfortable cockpit and active social schedule. El Toro An 8-foot pram sailed single-handedly, widely used by young Bay Area sailors and supported by an active local fleet. Open Fleet Other boat types are welcome to sail with the club and are scored under the Portsmouth Handicap System.

Fremont Sailing Club, Fremont, California
