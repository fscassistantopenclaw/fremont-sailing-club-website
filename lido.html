Fleets

Lido

The Lido 14 fleet combines approachable sailing, a comfortable cockpit, and an active social and sailing schedule.

The Lido 14 is a 14-foot, two-person boat with a mainsail and jib. The boat has a very comfortable cockpit and is easy and dry to sail.

The class has a very active social schedule as well as an active sailing schedule.
