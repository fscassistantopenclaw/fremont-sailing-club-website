The Club

Fremont Sailing Club is a family-oriented community of small boat sailors who race, cruise, camp, and sail together around the Bay Area.

The Fremont Sailing Club is a family oriented community of small boat sailors who have joined together to enjoy small boat sailing and related activities. These include regularly scheduled race events and special regattas at Shoreline Lake. There are also less stressful planned outings such as camp-outs and cruises on other waters.

Active fleets currently include the El Toro, Lido 14, and Open Fleet.

The club publishes a newsletter, the Windjammer, which announces the time and place of upcoming club events. Members may advertise in the Windjammer, or on the club website, without charge, to buy or sell sailboats and related equipment.
